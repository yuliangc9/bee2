Rampage Bee is committed to protecting the privacy of you and the personal information you may share with Rampage Bee or that Rampage Bee holds. This Policy (the "Policy") describes the information collected about you and Rampage Bee's practices for using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

Information Collected and Maintained

Rampage Bee collects and maintains your personal information when you:

  • (a) Contact us by phone, mail, or email, in person.
  • (b) Using our APP.

From time to time, we may collect personal information from you in ways not described above, subject to your express approval.

Confidentiality

All information provided between you and Rampage Bee is considered highly confidential and we employ appropriate security measures designed to protect it. While the information is kept confidential, it is shared with Rampage Bee's board members, staff, representatives, and professionals and with any of Rampage Bee's parent organizations or subsidiaries on a need-to-know basis and in compliance with all laws, regulations, court orders, or other governmental requests.

Records Retention and Destruction

Rampage Bee complies with all laws and local regulations and Rampage Bee's internal policies governing the retention, management, and destruction of donor information collected and maintained by Rampage Bee.

Accessing Your Personal Information

You can review the personal information that Rampage Bee has collected about you by contacting Rampage Bee as described in the "Contact Us" section below. However, please be aware that Rampage Bee may not accommodate a change request as we do not store any personal information provided by you.

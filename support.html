Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Why won't Rampage Bee open on my iPhone/iPad?
Try these steps first:
- Close the app completely (swipe up from the bottom of the screen and hold, then swipe the app away).
- Restart your device.
- Ensure your device is running iOS 14.0 or later (check in Settings > General > Software Update).
If the issue persists, contact our support team with your device model and iOS version.
2. How do I update Rampage Bee to the latest version?
Open the App Store app on your device, tap your profile icon in the top-right corner, scroll down to "Available Updates," and look for "Rampage Bee." Tap "Update" to install the latest version. If no update is listed, you already have the newest release.
3. Does Rampage Bee require an internet connection?
No—Rampage Bee works completely offline. All features (including gameplay, settings, and progress) operate locally on your device, so no internet connection is needed to use the app.
4. How do I restore my game progress in Rampage Bee?
Since Rampage Bee does not collect or store user data (including progress) on external servers, progress is saved locally on your device. If you switch devices or reset your current device, progress cannot be transferred. We recommend avoiding device resets if you want to keep your progress.
5. Is Rampage Bee compatible with my device?
Rampage Bee supports iPhones running iOS 14.0 or later, and iPads running iPadOS 14.0 or later. To check your device's compatibility, go to Settings > General > About (device model) and Settings > General > Software Update (OS version).
